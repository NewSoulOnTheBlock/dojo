About HomeGrow

Our mission is food independence for every household

Our Story

HomeGrow was born from a simple but urgent realization: the modern food supply chain is more fragile than most people think. When our founders experienced firsthand the empty shelves and weeks-long shortages during a regional crisis, they knew there had to be a better way.

What started as a personal project — growing food at home to protect their family — quickly became a passion. They saw that the technology, the knowledge, and the systems existed to make any home food-independent. The problem wasn't capability — it was accessibility.

HomeGrow was founded to bridge that gap. We combine premium growing systems with professional installation and hands-on education to make food independence not just possible, but effortless. Every home we equip is one more family that doesn't have to worry about what happens next.

Today, we've helped over 500 families across the country take control of their food supply. Our clients aren't just growing food — they're growing peace of mind, better nutrition, and a legacy of self-reliance for their families.
